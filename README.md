# result_excel
Excel based Result Processing System for Higher Secondary Colleges in Bangladesh (For the Group Science, Business, and Humanities) 

First of all, input students basic information 
Secondly, input of their subject list including selective and optional

Thirdly, Input of acquired marks of each students (Subjectwise separate sheet for each subjective teacher) 

Finally get 
        1. Combined students combined list including result status (Pass, Failed in 1 subject, Failed in 2 subjects, etc.)
        2. Students list who are passed in all subjects
        3. Students list who are failed in 1 subject
        4. Students list who are failed in 2 subject
        5. Students list who are failed in 3 subject
        6. Students list who are failed in 4 subject
        7. Students list who are failed in 5 subject
        8. Students list who are failed in 6 subject
        9. Combined tabulation sheet for all students including each of the students' complete grade sheet in 1 one page 
And Generate a PDF by one click for each student's grade sheet for printing.
